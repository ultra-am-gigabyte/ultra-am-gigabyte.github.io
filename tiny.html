What is Tiny Windows? These are third-party modified, ultra-stripped configurations of Windows. System extras like Microsoft Edge, Cortana, Xbox services, and built-in diagnostic tracking (telemetry) are removed to run efficiently on devices with as little as 2GB of RAM.

Tiny 11

A stripped-down modification of Windows 11. It removes the harsh hardware/TPM requirements, uses less than 10GB of storage space, and runs smoothly on older systems.
